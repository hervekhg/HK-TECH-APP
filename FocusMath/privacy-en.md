## Introduction

FocusMath ("the App") is published by HK TECH. We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have.

## 1. Data Collected

### Data stored locally on your device

FocusMath stores the following data **only on your device** (via Core Data and UserDefaults):

- **User profile**: date of birth, school level, preferred language
- **Progress**: XP, level, streak, game statistics (questions answered, accuracy rate, average time)
- **Preferences**: dark mode, sound, haptics, notification frequency
- **Daily Challenge**: today's score, personal best, challenge streak

### Data we do NOT collect

- We do **not** collect any personally identifiable information (name, email, address, phone number)
- We do **not** collect any geolocation data
- We do **not** use any third-party analytics tools (no Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
- We do **not** share any data with third parties

## 2. Use of Data

Data stored locally is used exclusively to:

- Personalize your learning experience (difficulty adapted to your level)
- Track your progress and statistics
- Display the iOS widget (streak, daily questions)
- Schedule reminder notifications (if enabled)

## 3. Data Sharing

**We do not share any data with third parties.** All data remains on your device.

## 4. Storage and Security

- All data is stored **locally** on your device via Core Data and UserDefaults
- Widget data uses a shared App Group between the app and the widget extension, still on your device
- No data is transmitted to any external server

## 5. User Rights

You can at any time:

- **View** your data via the Statistics / Dashboard screen in the app
- **Modify** your preferences via the Settings screen
- **Delete** all your data by uninstalling the app

## 6. Children

FocusMath is an educational app designed for children and adults. We do not collect any personal data, making the app compliant with regulations regarding the protection of minors (COPPA, GDPR).

## 7. Changes

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
